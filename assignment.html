Types of cancer

Cancer is not a single disease but a group of related conditions that occur when abnormal cells in the body grow uncontrollably and can spread to other parts of the body. Each type of cancer begins in a specific tissue or organ and behaves differently depending on where it starts, how fast it grows, and how it responds to treatment. Understanding the different types of cancer helps doctors, patients, and caregivers identify the right diagnosis, choose effective treatment plans, and improve overall outcomes.
